About me

Hi good people! Welcome to My Site!

This is Mir Rafiul Ahmed. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant in one of the leading economic research institutes of Bangladesh, South Asian Networking on Economic Modeling (SANEM).

I am a diligent Economics graduate student with a flair for creative thinking and a strong work ethic, possess compelling analytical and research-driven abilities, coupled with concise communication skills for effective presentations with proficiency in digital content creation, aiming to establish a fulfilling career in academia and policymaking while actively contributing to development

My research interests include (but are not limited to) the following:

  • Development Economics
  • Experimental & Behavioral Economics
  • Financial Economics
  • Gender Studies

I have completed my Bachelor of Social Science in Economics from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Sylhet, Bangladesh in May 2023. I worked under Mr. Gias Uddin Khan, Associate Professor, SUST for my final year project for 6 months from July 2022 to December 2023.

Prior to joining SANEM, I have also worked on different research projects for the SUST Research Center. Apart from that, I have also worked as a Research Intern in SANEM.

I have also initiated Econ Insider and worked as a founding executive director. Econ Insider is a non-partisan youth-led organisation for economics enthusiasts with a motive to drive young minds towards economic research through arranging economic discussions, workshops and contests.

You can explore the details about me and my work through this website. Feel free to get in touch with me for any queries.
