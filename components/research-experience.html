Publications & Research Experiences

Research Outputs

  1. Prediction of Seismic Effect on Structure using Deep Learning

    Type: Undergraduate Thesis
    Status: Under Review
    Developed deep learning models (cGAN & ANN) to predict von Mises stress fields and maximum stress under vertical and lateral loading.

  2. Comparative Analysis of Object Detection Models for Surface Water Pollution Detection

    Status: Ongoing
    Comparative evaluation of five object detection methods for identifying floating pollutants in surface water using annotated images.

  3. Water Sanitation and Hygiene Survey of Basirpur Village, Sylhet

    Status: Ongoing
    Conducted field & lab testing (iron, arsenic, fecal coliform) and analyzed survey data to assess household sanitation practices.

  4. Public Perception on Quarantine during the COVID-19 Outbreak in Bangladesh

    DOI: 10.29333/jcei/11703
    Community survey-based statistical analysis highlighting public responses and perceptions during the COVID-19 pandemic.

  5. Archetype of Energy Generation from Concentrated Solar Power & Thermal Gradient

    DOI: 10.1109/ICDRET60388.2024.10503636
    Presented a renewable energy system using thermal gradient created by concentrated solar heating of water bodies.

  6. IoT-Based Smart Vault Security & Monitoring System with Zero UI

    DOI: 10.1109/ICREST57604.2023.10070057
    Designed a multi-layered, touchless security system with intelligent monitoring using IoT technologies.

  7. A Novel Archetype of Energy Generation from Human Footsteps

    DOI: 10.36227/techrxiv.14998014.v1
